Sumanth was a Postdoc in the lab from April 2022-2026. Sumanth did his graduate work at the University of Mysore focused on how innate immune cells and the acute phase proteins regulate inflammation. Prior to joining our lab, Sumanth worked as a postdoc in Dr. Anandi Krishnan’s lab at Stanford University on molecular pathogenesis of blood cancers. He joined the Kaner lab at Weill Cornell as a postdoc after leaving the lab.
